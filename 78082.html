DDOS Attacks and Website Defacement

A Distributed Denial of Service (DDOS) attack floods a network or server with massive traffic to overload resources, causing downtime and disruption.

Defacement attacks alter a website's content, typically replacing it with unauthorized messages, graphics, or warnings.

Both attacks compromise availability, trust, and integrity of online services.

Stay vigilant, implement proper security measures, and monitor traffic for unusual spikes!
